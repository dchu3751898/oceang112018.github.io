My Project Idea

My project idea is a Grocery Shopping App on mobile phones. The Grocery Shopping is an application that allow users to buy groceries online without having them to actually go to the supermarket. Users will need to login into the system before they can view any groceries and add them to their shopping cart. After they're done, they can place the order by making a safe payment via Debit Card, Credit Card or PayPal. The orders will be recorded in a wide server in the web. This application is available in the AppStore and also Google PlayStore.

I came out with this idea when I was doing grocery shopping myself. I was lazy to walk out and buy the groceries. So I thought to myself, what if there is an application that allow users to buy groceries online and collect it whenever they like or even get it delivered to their home. So I went home and search for this type of applications. There was not much online so I told myself I wanted to create this application in the future. Besides that, my parents always have to walk a certain distance to buy groceries when I'm in school. After walking that far, they will feel tired. So I think this application will come in handy in the future and it will help many people save time and also save energy.

Description of my project idea

This application name will be Smart Market. It will be programmed with a user-friendly and eye-catching interface. This application will allow the users to buy and place an order of groceries through the application. This application will be connected to many supermarkets such as Coles, Woolworths, Metro, Asian groceries shop and many more. The way this application works is that when the users open the application, they will be asked to login into an account or sign up. After that, it will bring them to a page of selecting a language they would like to use. After selecting the language, the application will show different supermarkets. This is where the users will choose which supermarket they would like to do the grocery shopping at. Upon selecting the supermarket, they can do their groceries online. After finishing ordering, they will be a button at the bottom right saying "Add to Cart". Once the user selects "Add to Cart", the order will be saved and stored securely in a server. Then, the user will be asked to key in their Credit Card details, or any type of payment choice they prefer. After finishing the payment step, there will be an estimated time for the groceries to be ready for collection. If the user is busy to collect, they is a delivery option. It will cost a normal rate of $5 AUD. If the user signs up for premium membership of the application, the delivery will be free of charge. The premium membership will cost $10 AUD per month.

This application target users of age 18 or above. This is because it requires a type of payment information in order to sign up. Users below the age of 18 can still use the application provided their parents or guardian give them access and permission to the the credit card, debit card or the type of payment they are using.

This application has many advantages but we found one disadvantage that might affect the order. The advantages are the users do not have to wait in long queues to buy groceries. Secondly, users can purchase the groceries anytime and anywhere they like. This application does not require users to go to the supermarket. This application is available for free download as long as your mobile phone has App Store or Google Play Store. This application is also easy to navigate and easy to access. All the details and buttons will be clearly shown for users to click. This will prevent errors or users from getting lost in the application. The only disadvantage is that the quality of the product may differ from users choosing it at the store.
