Scope and Limits

Scope:

Making a few sets of customize Alexa instruction for students.

Limits:

Due to our limited knowledge of coding we’re only able to create a handful of basic features., anything that is complex like say a note taker where Alexa takes a recording and turn it into notes. The limitation of current echo is that they’re only able to take in and store short phrases.

Features:

As for now our main target would be university students, the possible features to include would be reviewing notes and quizzing students, school calendar reminder, daily random quote of the day to start day with.
